# Acme Corp — Refund and Return Policy

## Overview

This document outlines the refund and return policy for all products purchased through Acme Corp's online store and physical locations.

## Eligibility

Products are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase provided they are:
- In their original, unopened packaging
- Accompanied by the original receipt or order confirmation email
- Not listed in the non-refundable items section below

## Process

To initiate a return, contact our support team at support@acme.example.com with your order number and reason for return. A prepaid shipping label will be emailed within 2 business days.

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the returned item. The amount is credited back to the original payment method.

## Non-refundable Items

The following items cannot be returned or refunded:
- Digital downloads and software licenses
- Perishable goods
- Custom-engraved or personalised items
- Gift cards

## Exchanges

If you received a defective item, we will send a replacement at no extra cost. Contact support within 14 days of delivery with a photo of the defect.

## Shipping Costs

Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item shipped, manufacturing defect).

## Contact

For any questions, reach us at support@acme.example.com or call +1-800-ACME-123 (Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm EST).
